WELCOME TO THE CAT FETERIA

Our cat-feteria is a coffee shop where guests can come, enjoy the company of our cats over a drink and experience the benefit of some kitty therapy. Some of our amazing cat-feteria cats are available for adoption so we are hoping we can find forever homes for these fur babies.

AMENITIES

Cat wall furniture is a collection of cat shelf, or cat perch mix and match with wall mounted cat trees.

Coffee is a beverage brewed from the roasted and ground seeds of the tropical evergreen coffee plant.

Sure it's a place to rest, but a cat house can be so much more! Look for bonuses like plush hanging toys.

Cat-feteria interior has the ability to engage your guests, increase comfort, and urge them to become loyal patrons.

Guidelines and Rules

For the health of our cats, please sanitize your hands before entering the cat lounge.

All shoes must be covered with a film prior to entering the cat lounge to reduce the spreading of dirt and germs into the cat area.

Cats need to stay strictly in their designated area, please be aware that you do not accidently allow any of the cats into the food preparation area.

Please do not pick up the cats. Let the cat come to you for a stroke and a snuggle.
ABOUT THE CAT FETERIA

Many of our cats are pedigree because their calm nature is well suited for such an environment. Many are also coming from owners who were no longer able to take care of them or rescued from a hoarding situation. All of The Cat Café cats have their health checked, are fully vaccinated, neutered, microchipped and free from skin diseases and parasites.
